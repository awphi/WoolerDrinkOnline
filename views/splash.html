Party Mode

Designed to get you drunk quick but ween you off towards the end - i.e. the 'Social lubricant' mode.

Standard Mode

Humble but still fun, drinking ramps up but at a gradually slower pace.

Brutal Mode

Gets you exponentially more and more smashed (NOT for lightweights).
